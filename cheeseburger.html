Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

Craving a cheeseburger but don't want to order takeout? This recipe is perfect for you!

Enjoy yourself a homemade cheeseburger catered to your own cravings!

Ingredients

  • Potato buns
  • Ground beef patty
  • Sliced cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Seasoning of your choice
  • Spray oil

Steps

  1. Prepare your lettuce by peeling leaves from the head.
  2. Apply and rub your seasoning on both sides of the ground beef patty.
  3. Set your cast iron pan to medium-high heat and spray the pan with oil.
  4. When your pan is hot, add your burger patty to the pan.
  5. Leave the patty on each side according to your preference of wellness.
  6. Once the patty is almost finished, add one slice of cheese onto the patty.
  7. Remove the patty from the pain and place it onto a potato bun.
  8. Add a leaf of lettuce onto the patty and top it off with the other bun.
