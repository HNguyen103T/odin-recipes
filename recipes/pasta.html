Pasta Recipe

Description

Craving something zesty for dinner? Look no further!

This pasta recipe will provide you a fulfilling meal that's easy to package!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of penne pasta
  • 24 oz of pasta sauce
  • 1 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 can of diced tomato
  • 1 whole yellow onion
  • Seasonings
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Dice your yellow onion into appropriate sizes.
  2. Fill a pot of water and began boiling.
  3. As the water is heating up, add an appropriate amount of salt.
  4. Once the water is boiling, add renne pasta to the pot and boil until al dente.
  5. Remove pasta from water and rinse with cold water.
  6. Set another pan to medium heat and add an appropriate amount of oil.
  7. Once the pan is hot, add ground beef to the pan and cook until brown.
  8. As the ground beef is almost finished cooking, add diced onions to the pan and cook until golden.
  9. Add pasta sauce and diced tomato into the pan and mix.
  10. Leave the pan to simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  11. Plate cooked pasta and add pasta sauce on top.
