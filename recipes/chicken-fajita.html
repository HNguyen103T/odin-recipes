Chicken Fajita Recipe

Description

Want a healthy balance of protein and vegetables with a touch of spice?

Try this recipe for chicken fajita and keep yourself set for some few days!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Red bell pepper
  • Green bell pepper
  • Yellow bell pepper
  • Yellow onion
  • Mexican spice blend

Steps

  1. Slice each pepper into strips.
  2. Slice the onion into cuts.
  3. Slice the chicken breast into either slices or chunks.
  4. Add Mexican spice blend onto chicken and mix well.
  5. Heat a pan to medium heat and add chicken; cook thoroughly.
  6. As the chicken is almost cooked, mix in vegetables and cook thoroughly.
